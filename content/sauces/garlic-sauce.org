| Amount | Ingredient   |
|--------+--------------|
| 2 tbsp | mayonnaise   |
| 2 tbsp | yoghurt      |
| 1      | clove garlic |
|        | bieslook     |
|        | peterselie   |
|--------+--------------|

1. With the back of a knife, crush the garlic with a pinch of salt into a paste.
2. Mix garlic, mayonnaise and yoghurt. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
